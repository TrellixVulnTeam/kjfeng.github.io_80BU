About

Hey, I’m Kevin. I like making things and learning cool stuff along the way.

I’m a junior studying Computer Science at Princeton University and also a design aficionado. I see the technical side as a toolbox to realize solutions and visions, allowing great ideas to reach and benefit users. I see design as a fundamental problem solving framework and a productive mediator of discussions on ideas, often providing an important humane and ethical approaches that may be dropped otherwise. I strive to embody both worlds in my work.
