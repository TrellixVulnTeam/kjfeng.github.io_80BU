PUB Album Cover

Illustrator
Fall 2019

In the fall of 2019, the Princeton University band celebrated its 100th anniversary, complete with the release of a commemorative album. As a band member, I had the opportunity to design the cover for the CD album in collaboration with Soon Il Higashino '20.

The agreed title of the Album, "Plaid to the Bone", was a reference to "Bad to the Bone", a 1982 blues rock song by George Thorogood, as well as the iconic orange and black plaid blazers that are a part of the band's uniform. We thought a wild west theme with some sort of a surreal element (such as flying penny-farthing bicycles) would be apt to capture the adventurous and free-spirited of the band.
